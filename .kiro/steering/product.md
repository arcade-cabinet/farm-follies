# Farm Follies - Product Overview

Farm Follies is a physics-based stacking arcade game where players catch barnyard animals flung from a tornado, stack them on a farmer's head, and bank them before the tower topples.

## Core Loop
Catch Animals → Stack Grows → Wobble Increases → Bank or Risk → Repeat

## Key Features
- 9 animal types (chicken, duck, pig, goat, sheep, cow, goose, horse, rooster) with normal and special variants
- Wobble physics with collapse risk based on stack height and movement
- Banking system to secure points before toppling
- Power-ups: hay bale (life), golden egg (2x points), water trough (magnet), salt lick (invincibility), corn feed (merge stack)
- YUKA-powered AI director that adapts difficulty to player skill
- Cross-platform: Web, Android, iOS, Desktop (Electron)

## Game Modes
- Endless (default), Time Attack, Zen, Boss Rush

## Target Experience
- Immediately understandable controls (drag to move)
- Easy to play, hard to master
- Satisfying physics feedback
- Mobile-friendly with responsive scaling
